What is Starknet?

Starknet is a decentralized layer-2 network that enables Ethereum to scale securely and dapps to achieve unlimited scale for transactions and computation.

What is Starknet?

How does Starknet scale Ethereum?

Starknet uses math and cryptography to securely scale Ethereum. It operates as a Validity-rollup (or ZK-rollup): it "bundles" many transactions and "rolls" them to Ethereum as a single transaction.
